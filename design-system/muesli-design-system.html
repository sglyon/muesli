M-shaped waveform rendered as rounded bars. The full app icon uses ~21 bars forming a symmetrical "M" pattern with two peaks and a central valley. Simplified 9-bar preset for small contexts: [0.45, 0.65, 0.90, 1.0, 0.45, 1.0, 0.90, 0.65, 0.45]
